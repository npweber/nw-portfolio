About Me

I'm a Contract Minecraft Mod & Plugin Developer at Candor Studios, and I'm also a part-time Support Guide at the Naslund & Naslund Foundation. And outside of work, I'm a huge grand strategy gamer.

I graduated from CSU Channel Islands with an honors degree in Computer Science. However, I prefer to say that I learned about software development as a Freelance Minecraft Plugin Developer in high school. After graduating, I worked as a Contract Backend Engineer at ServiceMob, a data analytics company, for six months, where I re-engineered their data input pipeline to improve performance efficiency. Unfortunately, ServiceMob didn't permanently hire me onto the team due to a lack of funding, but they strongly recommended me to "any software team."

I love completing software projects of all kinds, but most of all, developing creative ideas in Minecraft. That's why I pivoted back to Minecraft plugin development after holding a Software Engineering role and am now seeking full-time employment in a Minecraft development studio. I've gained extensive experience with Minecraft over the years, so if you're looking for a skilled Minecraft developer, I can fill the role. Minecraft development is where my career started.
